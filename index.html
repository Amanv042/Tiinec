Welcome to TIINEC

The Innovative Institute for Natural Resources, Environment and Community

Our team of expert researchers brings a wealth of experience and knowledge from a diverse range of fields, including ecology, hydrology, social sciences, engineering, and more. With a dedicated full-time staff and professional strength in natural resources management, environmental management solution, and geo-informatics among others, TIINEC has the intellectual as well as infrastructure capacity to develop methods, tools and provide solutions. We work closely with local communities, government agencies, and other stakeholders to ensure that our research is relevant, practical, and effective.

Our mission is to promote sustainable development, environmental stewardship, and community empowerment through innovative research, education, and outreach. We believe that the health of our natural resources is intimately linked with the well-being of our communities.

Thank you for visiting our website, and we invite you to explore our activities, programs, and resources.

About us

TIINEC is a leading research group that focuses on promoting sustainable development, environmental stewardship, and community empowerment. Our team of expert researchers is dedicated to addressing the complex challenges facing our world today, and we strive to provide innovative solutions that are both scientifically sound and socially just. Our research focuses on a wide range of topics, including sustainable land use, resource management, climate change adaptation and mitigation, and environmental policy. We use cutting-edge research methods and technologies to gather data, analyze results, and develop solutions that promote sustainability.

TIINEC is focused to provide services in regard to the changes occurring in environment and natural resources and climate change related issues using geospatial tools, information platform solutions, field-based studies, etc. A professional and skilled team in the TIINEC believes in technology-driven empowerment of community with environment-centric solutions to the people. We use state-of-the-art tools and techniques with greater emphasis on GIS, GPS, remote sensing, information and communication.

TIINEC is committed to making a positive impact on our world. We believe that through collaboration, innovation, and dedication, we can create a more sustainable and just future for all. Our team of expert researchers and staff is dedicated to advancing the field of sustainability and environmental stewardship through cutting-edge research and education.

Services

TIINEC has the capacity for applications of the remote sensing using aerial and space (satellite) images, environmental statistics, spatial data analysis/modeling, geostatistics, spatial decision support system, network analysis, 3D-GIS, P-GIS and web-based solutions.

Map Service System
We process and analyze wide range of spatial and non-spatial data for the desktop systems and web applications with our map service systems.
Vitual Representation of Data
We create custom map design solutions with special attention to uncluttered and clean representation, visualization and solution-oriented database.
Remote Sensing Satellite
We provide services on remote sensing and GIS related projects including interpretation and processing of satellite remote sensing data.
Expertise
We have expertise in data conversion, digital spatial database creation and analysis, map production, ground truth and other data collection, surveying and customizing, and publishing these as web-service and information base.

These are particularly suited for presentations, analyses, interpretations and decision-making. TIINEC maintains a close relationship with academic institutions, scientists working in esteemed intuitions; professors of international repute, professionals and practitioners working in international organizations for timely advice, guidance and feedbacks.

With a handful of experience and knowledge it has delivered the best available solutions both scientifically sound and socially just.
